about DIOR

Françoise Dior (niece) Christian Dior (French: [kʁistjɑ̃ djɔʁ]; 21 January 1905 – 23 October 1957) was a French fashion designer

best known as the founder of one of the world's top fashion houses, also called Christian Dior, which is now owned by Groupe Arnault.

His fashion houses are now all around the world.
